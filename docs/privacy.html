PRIVACY

Cians Analytics places the utmost importance on preserving and respecting your privacy. We do not rent, sell, or share your personal information with any person or nonaffiliated companies, except to provide products or services you’ve requested and only with your explicit permission.

We may sometimes collect anonymous information from visits to our sites to help us provide better customer service. For example, we may keep track of the domains from which people visit, and we may also measure visitor activity on Cians Analytics’ website; however, we do so in ways that keep the information of visitors anonymous.

Cians Analytics, along with its affiliates and vendors, may use this data to analyze trends and statistics, and provide better customer service. We maintain the highest levels of confidentiality for this information, and our affiliates and vendors follow the same high levels of privacy.

This anonymous information is used and analyzed only at an aggregate level to help us understand trends and patterns. None of this information is reviewed at an individual level. If you do not want your transaction details used in this manner, you may prevent it by either disabling your cookies or opting-out on the download page.

We limit access to your personal information to employees who reasonably need to come in contact with that information in order to complete their daily operations, and/or to provide products or services to you. We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards in place to protect your personal information.

Cians Analytics may update this policy at any time. We will notify you about any significant changes in the way we treat personal information.
